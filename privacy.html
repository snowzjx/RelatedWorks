Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

RelatedWorks does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Data Storage

All your projects, papers, annotations, and PDFs are stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud Drive account. We have no access to this data.

AI Backends

If you choose to use Google Gemini, your paper text and annotations are sent to Google's API for processing. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy. If you use Ollama, all processing happens locally on your device and no data leaves your machine.

iCloud Sync

When iCloud sync is enabled, your data is stored in your personal iCloud Drive. Apple's privacy policy applies.

Analytics

RelatedWorks does not use any analytics, tracking, or crash reporting services.
